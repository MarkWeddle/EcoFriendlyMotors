Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

EcoFriendlyMotors.com ("we", "us", "this website") is an independent information website that helps consumers compare electric vehicles, home charging solutions and EV insurance. This Privacy Policy explains what information is — and is not — collected when you visit this website, and how your privacy is protected.

Information we do not collect

This website is informational only. We keep visitor data collection to the practical minimum, and in particular:

  • No customer accounts are created. There is no login, sign-up or membership functionality anywhere on this website.
  • No personal information is collected through forms. This website contains no contact forms, inquiry forms or data-capture forms of any kind.
  • No payments are processed. Nothing is sold on this website and no payment, billing or financial information is ever requested or handled by us.
  • No newsletters are offered. We do not operate mailing lists and we will never send you marketing email, because we do not collect email addresses.
  • No visitor registration is required. Every page of this website is freely accessible without providing any personal details.

Information collected automatically

Like almost every website, the server that hosts this website may automatically record standard technical information when a page is requested, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, the pages viewed and the date and time of your visit. This information is used only in aggregate for security, performance and to understand which guides are most useful. It is not used to identify individual visitors and is not combined with any other data about you.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. This website does not require cookies to function, and we do not use cookies to collect personal information about you. However, cookies may be set in two situations:

  • Analytics. If aggregate analytics are used to measure page visits, the analytics provider may set cookies to distinguish repeat visits. Any such measurement is anonymous and used only to improve the website.
  • Affiliate and third-party links. When you click a link to a partner website, that website may set its own cookies (see "Affiliate links" below).

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking cookies will not prevent you from using any part of this website.

Affiliate links

This website contains affiliate links. If you click a link to one of our partners and go on to make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you, as described in our Affiliate Disclosure. When you click an affiliate link, the partner or an affiliate network may set a cookie in your browser so the referral can be attributed to this website. That cookie is created and controlled by the partner, not by us, and we never receive your personal or payment details from any purchase you make on a partner's website.

Third-party websites

Links on this website lead to manufacturer, retailer, insurer and other third-party websites. Those websites operate under their own privacy policies, which we do not control and are not responsible for. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit after leaving this one.

Embedded content

All images on this website are hosted by us. Web fonts may be served by a third-party font service; when your browser loads a font file, that service receives a standard web request including your IP address. No other content on this website is loaded from third-party sources.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for adults researching vehicle purchases. It is not directed at children, and because no personal information is collected through the website, no personal information about children is knowingly collected or stored.

Your privacy rights

Because this website does not create accounts, operate forms, process payments or maintain mailing lists, we do not hold personal profiles about individual visitors that could be accessed, corrected or deleted. Where anonymous, aggregate analytics are used, they cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website or to applicable law. Any revised policy will be published on this page with an updated date, and continued use of the website after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
